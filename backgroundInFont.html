Airbnb

Airbnb is the world's largest accommodation sharing service that began in August 2008. You can rent all the spaces that people can stay in, such as your own room, house, villa, etc. As of 2013, accommodation in 34,800 places in 192 countries is being mediated, and reservations are being made for every two seconds.
